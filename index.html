Privacy Policy

HabitNode — Habit Tracker App

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how HabitNode ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), developed by VHIR, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

By downloading, installing, or using HabitNode, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

🔐 Key Point: HabitNode stores ALL your data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When using HabitNode, you may provide the following information, which is stored locally on your device only:

  • Profile Information: Display name and avatar emoji (optional, stored locally)
  • Habit Data: Habit names, descriptions, categories, schedules, goals, priorities, notes, and completion records
  • Missed Habit Reasons: When you miss a habit, you may optionally provide a reason (stored locally for smart reminders)
  • Achievement Data: Badges earned, XP points, level progress, streaks
  • App Preferences: Theme mode, notification settings, sound settings, alarm configurations

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App itself does NOT collect any personal information automatically. However, the following third-party services integrated in the App may collect certain data:

Service Provider Purpose Data Collected
Google AdMob Google LLC Display banner advertisements Advertising ID, device info, IP address (anonymized)
Unity Ads Unity Technologies Display interstitial & rewarded advertisements Advertising ID, device info, ad interaction data
Google Play Billing Google LLC Process in-app purchases (Pro subscription) Purchase transaction data (handled by Google)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name, email address, phone number, or any personal contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS messages
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, face recognition)
  • Financial information or payment card details (handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Health or fitness data from device sensors

2. How We Use Information

All habit data you create is used exclusively on your device for the following purposes:

  • Habit Tracking: To display, manage, and track your daily habits and routines
  • Notifications & Alarms: To send you scheduled reminders and full-screen alarms for your habits
  • Smart Reminders: To generate personalized reminder messages based on your missed habit reasons (processed locally, never sent to any server)
  • Statistics: To calculate and display your completion rates, streaks, and progress charts
  • Badge & XP System: To track your achievements, award badges, calculate experience points, and determine your level
  • Text-to-Speech: To speak habit names and alarm descriptions aloud using your device's built-in TTS engine
  • Sound Effects: To play audio feedback for habit completions, badge unlocks, and other app interactions
  • Backup & Restore: To allow you to export and import your data as JSON files stored on your device
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements (free users only) through Google AdMob and Unity Ads
  • In-App Purchases: To process Pro subscription purchases through Google Play Billing
💡 Smart Reminders: When you provide a reason for missing a habit, the app generates a personalized notification message locally on your device. This data is NEVER transmitted to any external server or AI service.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Hive, a lightweight, encrypted-capable local database. Data is organized in three separate storage containers:

  • Habits Box: All habit data, completion records, missed reasons, and progress
  • Settings Box: App preferences, theme, notification settings, purchase status
  • Badges Box: Achievement data, XP points, badge unlock records, usage statistics

3.2 No Cloud Storage

HabitNode does NOT use any cloud services, remote databases, or external servers to store your data. We do not use Firebase, AWS, or any other cloud platform for data storage.

3.3 Data Security

Your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face unlock) on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Regularly backing up your habit data using the in-app backup feature

3.4 Backup Files

When you use the backup feature, your data is exported as a JSON file to your device's storage. This file contains your habits, settings, and achievement data. You are solely responsible for the security of exported backup files.

6. Advertising

HabitNode displays advertisements to free users through Google AdMob (banner ads) and Unity Ads (interstitial and rewarded video ads).

6.1 Ad Personalization

Ad networks may use your device's Advertising ID and other non-personal information to show relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • In-App: Upgrade to Pro to remove all advertisements

6.2 Ad Frequency

We implement responsible ad practices:

  • Interstitial ads are shown with reasonable frequency limits
  • Rewarded ads are shown only when the user voluntarily chooses to watch them
  • Banner ads are displayed at the bottom of the main screen
  • Pro subscribers see zero advertisements

9. Data Retention & Deletion

9.1 Retention

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. We do not retain any data on external servers.

9.2 Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual Habits: Delete specific habits from within the App
  • All Data: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the App removes ALL locally stored data permanently
⚠️ Warning: Uninstalling HabitNode will permanently delete all your habits, badges, streaks, and settings. Use the backup feature to save your data before uninstalling.

10. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

The only data that leaves your device is:

  • Anonymous advertising data sent to Google AdMob and Unity Ads (for ad serving)
  • Purchase transaction data sent to Google Play (for subscription processing)
  • Backup files that you manually export and choose to share
