Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Habit Flow (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Habit Flow mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Habit Flow may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Habit Data: habits, streaks, reminders, progress, categories, colors, and related app content created by you.
  • Device Information: limited technical information such as device type, OS version, crash data, and app performance data.
  • Purchase Information: subscription or premium purchase status through Google Play or Apple App Store.
  • Advertising Data: if ads are enabled, third-party ad providers such as Google AdMob may collect advertising-related identifiers and interaction data.
  • Notification Preferences: reminder settings and scheduled notification preferences.
  • Backup Files: if you export a backup, your selected habit data may be saved as a JSON file on your device or shared by you manually.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide habit tracking and reminder features
  • Save your progress and streaks locally
  • Restore your purchased premium status
  • Display advertisements (if applicable)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Generate analytics and statistics inside the app

3. Local Storage

Habit Flow primarily stores your habit data locally on your device using local storage technology such as Hive. This means your personal habit entries are generally not uploaded to our servers unless you manually export or share them.

4. Backup and Restore

Habit Flow may allow you to export your data as a JSON backup file. That file is controlled by you. If you share or upload the file, you are responsible for where it is sent and stored.

5. Third-Party Services

Habit Flow may use third-party services including but not limited to:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases (for subscriptions and premium access)
  • Firebase / crash or analytics tools (if enabled in future versions)
  • Device notification services

These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Advertising

If ads are shown in the app, ad providers may collect certain device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, and other technical data to deliver and improve advertising.

7. In-App Purchases

Premium purchases and subscriptions are handled by official app stores such as Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

8. Notifications

Habit Flow may request notification permissions in order to send habit reminders, summaries, or related alerts. You can disable notifications from your device settings or from within the app if available.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal habit data. We may share limited technical information with third-party providers only when necessary to operate app functionality such as ads, purchases, or app performance tools.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no storage method or transmission method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children’s Privacy

Habit Flow is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

12. Your Choices

  • You may delete habits and local data from inside the app
  • You may uninstall the app to remove locally stored data
  • You may disable notifications from device settings
  • You may choose not to purchase premium features
  • You may choose whether or not to export/share backups

13. International Users

If you use the app outside your country, you understand that information may be processed according to applicable platform or third-party provider systems.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
